What this service offers to you?

design

All the computers we build are fully adapted to the customer's specifications and preferences, which makes them unique. The client is kept informed about the construction process at all stages.

service

As the only available service, it offers the installation of a computer set with new and used parts, as well as the modernization of the existing one with the option of reducing the price by exchanging your old component for new ones or sending them back to you. The update aims to increase performance and visual changes to bring your PC to life. We also offer a post-warranty service.

warranty

The entire set is covered by the manufacturer's warranty for each component and it may be from 2 to even 5 years, as opposed to pre-build, where the warranty covers the entire computer and is 1 year. This allows for greater security and faster service without additional costs associated with the purchase of additional protection.

orders

To place an order, please send an inquiry with details, whether it is a new computer, an update, or our offer. If you choose the update option, please provide the current specification, what is to be changed and what amount you want to allocate. After sending the inquiry, I will present you with the best offer and alternative that you need to know about. Payment for the order is made in advance via PayPal. The time needed to build a computer varies from one week to two, depending on the project.
